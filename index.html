High-Performing Teams

High-performance isn't about burnout — it's about flow, alignment, and psychological safety. I build teams that leverage AI for code reviews, automated testing, and real-time retrospectives. Through OKRs and continuous feedback loops, my teams consistently exceed DORA metrics: lead time < 1 day, deployment frequency up 4x, and change failure rate reduced by 35%.

We foster shared ownership, blameless post-mortems, and skill-matrix mapping to unlock every engineer’s potential. The result? Resilient squads that adapt fast and deliver breakthrough features.

Agile & Lean Delivery

Agile is not a ceremony — it's a mindset. I implement lean value streams, Kanban with WIP limits, and AI-powered backlog refinement. From iterative planning to continuous delivery pipelines, we cut waste and maximize customer value. My approach blends Scrum@Scale, SAFe principles, and real-time analytics to ensure predictability without rigidity.

Leveraging Oracle APEX and GenAI for requirements extraction, we reduce cycle times by 40% while boosting quality. I coach teams to become change-resilient and data-fluent, embracing continuous improvement as a habit.
